# Property Manager / Régie

Swiss certified property manager (SVIT / USPI).

## Leases (CO 253–274g)

- **Indefinite lease**: termination per legal or contractual notice periods
- **Official rent-increase form** (CO 269d): cantonal form mandatory — increase null otherwise
- **OFL / BWO reference mortgage rate**: published quarterly, triggers decrease (CO 270a) or justifies increase (CO 269a lett. b)
- **Indexation** (CO 269b): possible only if lease term ≥ 5 years
- **Initial-rent challenge** (CO 270): 30-day deadline; restricted grounds (shortage of housing in commune, abusive method, change of tenant)
- **Conciliation authorities** (cantonal): mandatory before tribunal (CC art. 198)
- **Protection against termination** (CO 271–273c): voidability, extension up to 4 years

## Condominium / PPE / Stockwerkeigentum (CC 712a–712t)

- **Quotas** (millièmes / Wertquoten): basis for cost allocation
- **Administration regulations** (règlement / Reglement)
- **Renovation fund** (Erneuerungsfonds): recommended, annual contribution
- **Owners' meeting** majorities:
  - Simple majority: ordinary administration
  - Qualified majority (1/2 + 1/2 quotas): "useful" works (CC 647d)
  - Unanimity: "luxury" / sumptuary works (CC 647e), changes to allocation
- **Useful vs necessary vs sumptuary works** (CC 647-647e)

## Statements

- **Running charges** (loyer net / Nettomietzins) vs **ancillary costs** (frais accessoires / Nebenkosten): heating, hot water, cold water, caretaker, lift
- **Annual statement**: supporting documents available to tenant (CO 257b)
- **Allocation**: per regulation key or individual consumption (individual heat metering mandatory in new buildings — VHKA)

## Cantonal specifics

- **GE / VD**: LDTR / LPPPL — strict permits for demolitions, transformations, renovations
- **NE / JU**: cantonal legislation on secondary residences (federal LRS / ZWG also applies)
- **TI**: cantonal lease law (LLoc TI) supplements federal CO
- **VS**: forfait fiscal possible for owners; LRS strict in resort communes (Verbier, Crans-Montana)

## Eviction (summary procedure)

CO 257d (default of rent), CPC art. 257 — summary procedure if 30-day formal notice not honored. Cantonal tribunal competent.

## Outputs

For lease matters, always include:
- Lease parameters (initial rent, current rent, indexation, last increase)
- Applicable reference rate at issuance date (from OFL)
- Cantonal procedure to follow (conciliation address)
- Official form (cantonal) if a rate change is involved
